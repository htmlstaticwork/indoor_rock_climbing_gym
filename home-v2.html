HAVE QUESTIONS?

CLEARING THE BETA

New to climbing or switching gyms? We've answered the most common hurdles below. If you need more help, our team is always on hand.

Contact Support

Walk-ins are always welcome for casual climbing. However, intro classes and specific workshops should be booked 24 hours in advance via our app.

Comfortable, stretchy athletic wear. Avoid loose jewellery and long accessories. We provide professional climbing shoes for rental.

We welcome climbers from age 5 and up. Children under 12 must be supervised by an adult at all times unless enrolled in a coached youth session.

We reset 25% of the gym every Monday morning. Every route in the facility is fresh at least once per month.
