THE PINNACLE OF CHALLENGE

Lead climbing is the ultimate expression of skill and mental fortitude at Summit Ascent. Our dedicated lead walls feature 60-foot vertical climbs with aggressive overhangs and technical slab sections.

Safety First

Full certification required for all lead climbers. Daily inspections of draws and anchors.

Modern Gear

High-quality ropes and Petzl GriGri devices available for rentals.

WHAT TO EXPECT

Our lead wall is reset every Tuesday night, ensuring a fresh challenge for our elite members. From overhanging boulder-start leads to technical 40-meter endurance burns, we cater to all grades from 5.10a to 5.14c.

Anyone who has passed our lead certification test. If you're new to leading, we offer intensive 2-day courses to get you up to speed.

Testing involves demonstrating safe clipping technique, proper belaying, and successful catch/fall scenarios.

SERVICE INFO

  • Wall Height: 60 Ft
  • Draws: 25 Sets
  • Min. Grade: 5.10a
  • Peak Hours: 5PM - 9PM
